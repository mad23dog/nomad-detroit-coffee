Our Story

We founded Nomad Detroit Coffee in 2018 rooted in our shared love and curiosity of coffee and the journey it takes from earth to cup. As Metro Detroit's premier small-batch coffee roaster, we artistically roast in small batches ensuring a perfect brewed cup aimed to transport you to the corner of the world from which it originates. Specializing in single-origin coffee from Ethiopia, Guatemala, and Nicaragua, we bring Oakland County the finest beans roasted to perfection. Find us at the Royal Oak Farmers Market every Saturday morning, serving the communities of Royal Oak, Birmingham, Troy, Ferndale, and greater Detroit.

Our Coffee

Ethiopia Single-Origin Coffee

Single-origin Ethiopian coffee with vibrant blueberry sweetness and bright lemon citrus notes, reminiscent of a fresh-baked pound cake. Natural processed beans from the Wush Wush region.

Guatemala Single-Origin Coffee

Rich Guatemalan single-origin from Baja Verapaz with indulgent macadamia nut richness and sweet cookie-like finish. Washed process brings out clean, balanced flavors.

Nicaragua Single-Origin Coffee

Nicaraguan single-origin from Matagalpa featuring warm brown sugar sweetness, bright cherry acidity, and satisfying nutty undertones. Expertly washed for clarity and balance.

Vagabond Coffee Blend
