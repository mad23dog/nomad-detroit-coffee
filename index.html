Our Story

We founded Nomad Detroit Coffee in 2018 rooted in our shared love and curiosity of coffee and the journey it takes from earth to cup. Blending together craft and data we artistically roast in small batches ensuring a perfect brewed cup aimed to transport you to the corner of the world from which it originates.

Specializing in single-origin coffee from Ethiopia, Guatemala, and Nicaragua, we bring you the finest beans roasted to perfection. Visit us at the Royal Oak Farmers Market every Saturday morning.
